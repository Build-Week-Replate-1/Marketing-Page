About Replate

RePLate prevents food from being wasted. We connect restaurants, coffee shops, and fast food stores with food banks and other non-profits helping those who are in need of food and better nutrition. Our mission is to help better connect our community, promote conservation of resources, and make a less wasteful planet.

For Non-Profits

For Nonprofits, community managers can look in their community for participating businesses and schedule to pickup their extra food. In our easily navigable app you can communicate with businesses and see what types of extra food they have and find the best times to pickup excess food.

For Business Owners

If your business is looking for a way to give back to community, Replate is your spot. We understand that owning a restaurant is part business and part science and you always have extra food at the end of day. RePlate allows you to give back to those in need in the community and promote sustainability.
